Summary

Living with roommates is great, but managing chores, tasks, and shared responsibilities can lead to confusion and unnecessary conflict. Homie is a mobile app designed to bring clarity and fairness back to your shared household by automating task management, logging contributions, and improving communication.

Business Strategy

Customer Acquisition

  • Target shared households and student dorms
  • Promote via social media, university campaigns, and referrals
  • Simple onboarding for multiple household members

Customer Retention

  • Transparent logs & dashboards ensuring fairness and accountability
  • Notifications & reminders for task completion
  • Gamification: points, badges, streak rewards
  • Personalization: color-coded members, task categories, urgency labels

CRM View

  • New Users: Onboarding support to help them get started
  • Active Users: Rewards & engagement incentives
