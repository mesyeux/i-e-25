Summary of Homie

Homie is a mobile app that helps shared households organise chores clearly and fairly.
It solves confusion and conflict caused by unclear responsibilities.
Members can assign tasks, set deadlines, and receive reminders.
Completed tasks are logged to show contribution and progress.
A shared calendar and dashboard improve transparency and communication.
